Our Love Story

Welcome to our wedding website! We are so excited to be getting married and to share our lives with one another. We are thankful to those of you who have helped and supported us over the past five years, and we look forward to sharing this special moment with you!

I do!

Kerri Zaine

My mum used to tell me when she was a young girl she would always wonder who she would marry when she grew up. Naturally, when I was a young girl, I did the same. Little did I know that I had already met him and was going to be the luckiest girl in the world.

Alex is truly my better half. I am so fortunate to have met someone so kind, funny, generous, and caring. I could not have asked for a better partner. I have loved our adventures together over the past five years, and am so excited for the years to come, and can’t wait to have you by my side forever.

Me too!

Alex Brown

I still to this very day cannot believe I'm going to be marrying the girl next door!

Okay maybe she was a couple of blocks away, but still - there isn't a more caring, thoughtful, and not to mention beautiful person that I'd rather be with. She's my partner in crime, and there isn't a day that goes by where I don't think about how lucky I am to be with her and to share our lives together.

"I mean, what are you thinking about Jerry? Marriage? Family? ... They're prisons!" - Cosmo Kramer
1997

June: Grade 7 Grad Dance

Alex finally builds up the courage to ask his long time crush - Kerri Zaine to the grade 7 graduation dance. The result? Utter rejection.

Swallowing his pride, he attends the dance solo and learns a valuable lesson - ask earlier!

2002

December: Teenage Wasteland

It was grade 12, and the rejection of the grade 7 dance still haunts him, but he's 5 years older, wiser, and somewhat better looking what with all the braces being off and all. Despite suffering a broken leg in an unfortunate tree planting accident at Steveston Park, he rides up in his wheelchair after History 12 and asks Kerri to go out on a date.

His efforts are rewarded with a yes, and they enjoy a few dates together while watching movies and listening to Metallica in his wood-paneled jeep cherokee. Despite Alex having such a sweet ride, things did not quite work out. Would this really be the end for Kerri and Alex?

2010

February: Vancouver 2010

Kerri is living with Natalie, next to the Denny’s on Broadway. Natalie hosts a party and invites Alex - him and Kerri reconnect as friends. After many outings, and shared greasy Denny’s onion rings and laughing at Larry David, Natalie tells Kerri that it’s pretty obvious Alex likes her and to ask him out. Kerri is too shy to take the first step.

October: First Date: The Other Guys

One evening, Natalie takes Kerri’s phone and texts Alex on her behalf, asking if he’s interested in going on a date. He says yes, and plans their first official date.

Alex wants to make sure he really impresses Kerri, and knows she loves to laugh, so he takes her to see a classic Will Ferrell movie: The Other Guys.

2011

March: A Visit Down Under

Kerri is off travelling South East Asia, Australia, and New Zealand. While she is in Sydney, Alex decides to fly down under to see her and spend a week there. They enjoy the beaches, zoo, the streets, and some classic Australian nightlife.

Alex gets a sunburn. They learn that SPF 50 sunscreen is a necessity from now on.

May: Move #1 - Moving in Together

Kerri and Alex decide to move in together and find a wonderful little place in Vancouver, just off of Main street.

They would live here for a year and a half, and love Mt. Pleasant, but begin to feel cramped in their ‘large’ 250 sf. apartment.

December: First Family Vacation

Alex and Kerri are lucky to be whisked away by his family to beautiful Hawaii for their first Christmas away from home. They enjoy homemade delicious Mai Tai's on the patio, work on a few puzzles, and even rent a jeep for a day to explore all that Oahu has to offer.

Alex is homesick, and gets another sunburn. Kerri never wants to leave as her practicum looms in the near future.

2012

December: Family Vacation #2

This time the Zaine family whisks Alex and Kerri away to Maui for a winter vacation. They experience their first a luau, drive up 10,000 ft to the peak of Haleakala National Park, and go snorkelling in the bay. While snorkelling, Alex has a close encounter with a turtle, and runs out of the water to warn the others of this dangerous sea creature. Alex accidentally leaves the SPF 50 at home one morning and gets another sunburn.

August: Move #2

Kerri and Alex decide it is time to move farther east for something more spacious, so they find a place at King Edward and Knight. After several ‘interesting’ encounters with different neighbours on their floor they quickly learn that this isn’t the best place for them. Alex and Kerri decide to move west. They immediately find a new place across town in Kitsilano. They live below a wonderful family for a year, but the temptation to purchase a home for themselves begins to grow.

2013

August: Move #3 - Back to Steveston!

In an effort to save money for their upcoming down payment on a new apartment, Alex and Kerri move back to Steveston. A wonderfully generous couple, Debby and Doug Newton, Kerri’s childhood next door neighbours, welcome them into their home for an entire year.

It's a perfect fit as Kerri and Debby sew and watch TV in the evenings, while Alex and Doug do some hard core gaming in the other room.

2014

August: Move #4 - We're home owners!

Their new apartment has beautiful furnishings, and it's a great location, but sometimes life deals you a bad hand of cards and you have to cut your losses. Kerri and Alex search for a new home.

2015

June: Move #5 - Still home owners!

After the sale of their apartment, they end up finding a fantastic place in Fairview Slopes. It's everything they hoped for and finally time to lay down some roots!

August: Engaged!

After settling into their new home, Kerri returns on a Tuesday evening to find Alex in an apron, with the table set, two glasses of wine poured, a bocconcini salad, and a homemade dinner cooking on the stove. She is thrilled to find him happy in the kitchen. Moments later he is on one knee and proposes. Kerri says Yes!\

And so the Adventure
begins
