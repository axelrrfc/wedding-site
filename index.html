Alex Brown

I still to this very day cannot believe I'm going to be marrying the girl next door (sort of)!

Okay maybe she was a couple of blocks away, but still - there isn't a more caring, thoughtful, and not to mention beautiful person that I'd rather be with. She's my partner in crime, and there isn't a day that goes by where I don't think about how lucky I am to be with her and to share our lives together.

"I mean, what are you thinking about Jerry? Marriage? Family? ... They're prisons!" - Cosmo Kramer
1997

June: Grade 7 Grad Dance

Alex finally builds up the courage to ask his long time crush - Kerri Zaine to the grade 7 graduation dance. The result? Utter rejection.

Swallowing his pride, he attends the dance solo and learns a valuable lesson - ask earlier!

2002

December: Teenage Wasteland

It was grade 12, and the rejection of the grade 7 dance still haunts him, but he's 5 years older, wiser, and somewhat better looking what with all the braces being off and all. Despite suffering a broken leg in an unfortunate tree planting accident at Steveston Park, he rides up in his wheelchair after History 12 and asks Kerri to go out on a date.

His efforts are rewarded with a yes, and they enjoy a few dates together while watching movies and listening to Metallica in his wood-paneled jeep cherokee. Despite Alex having such a sweet ride, things did not quite work out. Would this really be the end for Kerri and Alex?

2010

February: Vancouver 2010

A reconnection through mutual friends brings them back together as friends... But for how long?

October: First Date: The Other Guys

After months of fantastic friendship, they decide that its time to take things to the next level - a date.

Alex wants to make sure he really impresses Kerri, so he takes her to see a classic Will Ferrel movie: The Other Guys.

2011

Mar: A Visit Down Under

While Kerri is traveling in Australia, Alex decides to fly down under to see her and spend a week in Sydney.

They enjoy the beaches, the zoo, and some classic Australian nightlife.

Alex gets a sun burn.

May: Move #1 - Moving in Together

Kerri and Alex finally decide to move in together and find a wonderful place in Vancouver, just off of Main street.

They would live here for 14 months before deciding to move again. But little did they know what the next place would be like!

Dec: The First Vacation

Their first official vacation together is to Hawaii with Alex's family.

They enjoy Mai Tai's on the patio and even rent a jeep for a day to explore all that Oahu has to offer.

Alex gets a sun burn.

2012

Aug: Move #2

It's time to leave their Main street apartment, so they find a nice place at King Edward an Knight.

Sep: Move #3 - Whoops!

Turns out, it's a weird place! They immediately find a new place across town in Kitsilano.

They live below a wonderful family for an entire year, but the temptation to purchase a home for themselves is too great.

2013

Aug: Move #3 - Back to Steveston!

In an effort to save up money for their upcoming apartment purchase, Alex and Kerri move in with Doug and Debbie Newton in Steveston.

It's a fantastic fit as Kerri and Debbie sew and watch TV in the evenings, while Alex and Doug do some hard core gaming in the other room.

2014

Aug: Move #4 - We're home owners!

While the nightly LANs are fantastic, all good things must come to an end. It's time to take possession of their new house though!

They pack up their things, and say their tough goodbyes to the Newtons who were monumental hosts for an entire year.

2015

June: Move #5 - Still home owners!

Their new apartment is nice, but sometimes life gives you a bad hand of cards and you have to cut your losses.

They find a fantastic new place in Fairview Slopes. It's everything they hoped and it's finally time to lay down some roots!

Aug: Engaged!

With their final (for now) move in place, there was only one thing to do...

And so the Adventure
